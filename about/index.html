About Me

Hi, I am Rohan. I read and write code. I frequently read about physics, energy and climate change. After getting depressed enough of the latter, I read fiction or annoy my friends with my ideas or just code.

I like to play table tennis, cricket and chess. I also bought a harmonica. Keep your hands on ears if you see me holding it. And yeah, I maintain a blog, which is so devoid of activity that it should have cobwebs on it.

On the days with clear skies, I take out my telescope and observe star clusters and nebulae. I am trying to go through the Messier catalog.
